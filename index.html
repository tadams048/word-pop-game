How to Play

Find as many words as possible in the 4x4 grid of letters.

  • Words must be at least 3 letters long
  • Letters must be adjacent (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally)
  • The same letter cube cannot be used more than once in a word
  • Scoring: 3 letters = 1 point, 4 letters = 1 point, 5 letters = 2 points, 6 letters = 3 points, 7 letters = 5 points, 8+ letters = 11 points
  • You have 1 minute to find as many words as possible
  • A new puzzle is available every day
